Introduction

Math Tools in C is a collection of small C programs to solve simple mathematical problems!

Program List

  • Amicable Numbers
  • Arithmetic Progressions
  • Armstrong Numbers
  • Binomial Expansion
  • Complex Numbers
  • Decimal-Binary Conversion
  • Factorial
  • Factors of a number
  • Fibonacci
  • HCF (Highest Common Factor)
  • LCM (Lowest Common Multiple)
  • Linear Curve Fitting
  • Matrices
  • Multiples of a number
  • nCr (Combination)
  • nPr (Permutation)
  • Perfect Numbers
  • Prime Numbers
  • Quadratic Equations
  • Reverse a number
